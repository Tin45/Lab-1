Socit fest Ok soo SOCIT fest happened and flew by pretty fast. However, it wasn’t as lively as I’d expect it to be. Because however good the topics for discussion in the auditorium were, if you’re just gonna stand in front of the stage and really just read whats on display. Then its not really worth listening to, that’s why a lot of people can be spotted sleeping while the less than entertaining topics were being presented. I listened nonetheless, however I expected the presenter to be more interactive with their audience, have us watch videos, show memes, or at least try to relate to us teens. We have a different approach on entertainment and other serious topics. But in all honesty I still appreciated the activities that we attended.
